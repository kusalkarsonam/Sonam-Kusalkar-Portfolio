Who is Sonam?

A frontend developer who treats every screen like a drawing set — measured, labelled, and built to hold up under real use. I care about the inch between "it works" and "it feels right."

My process starts on paper: structure, hierarchy, and states, before a single line of CSS. That habit is why this page looks the way it does — every section here is a sheet in the set.

Off-screen, I'm usually breaking down someone else's interface to see how it was built, then rebuilding a cleaner version for practice.
